LINE FOLLOWER ROBOT


Objective:

The Objective of this contest is for a Robot to follow a Black Line without losing the time. The Robot has to complete the course in the shortest period(best of three) of time while accurately tracking the course line from start to finish.





Rules

ROBO-WARFARE


Objective:

The Objective of this contest is to build a Robot whose task is to damage its opponent Robot and gain as much points as it can for the first 4 minutes and if the points are on tie, then an additional time limit of 2 minutes will be given to both the robots to push each other out of the ring. Different Robots will compete 1 vs 1 against each other.
