Welcome to TECHCRACY 2018

THE ANNUAL TECHNICAL EXTRAVAGANZA

ONPHAORI - Hands towards HUMANITY

29th, 30th & 31st October 2018




TECHNO-EXHIBITION

JOUGA - The Rise:

In Techcracy’18, we aim to carry forward the motive that has been started in Techcracy 2014 to apply technology for humanity. And humanity always doesn’t mean sympathy. Responsibility, patriotism are also important forms of humanity. As the saying goes, “Helping hands are better than praying hands”. In this participants will make models and PowerPoint presentation (optional) on projects which aims at the development of this region. The Development will mainly aim at solving the unemployment problems and unconventional use of energy.

Rules:

    -Each group can have a maximum of 5 members.

    -Rewards will be given to best 3 groups.

    -Certificate of participation will be given to each group.

Prize:

1st prize: Rs. 5000+Certificate; 2nd prize: Rs.3000+Certificate; 3rd prize: Rs. 1500+Certificate

Date: 29th of October 2018 (9 am onwards)

DU-FANG –The Dawn:

Mugging up from books and jotting in exam are not all of engineering. We implement our theoretical knowledge in real life. We create, implement, modify. We are pioneers. Du-Fang, the dawn is the fresh start for a change in this regard. It is the grand technical model exhibition show by CIT, Kokrajhar.

Rules:

    -Models can be of any technical stream or topic.

    -A group can have 3-5 members.

    -Certificate of participants will be provided to each group.

    -New ideas, inventions, models on recent technological advances are preferred.

Prize:

1st prize: Rs. 5000+Certificate; 2nd prize: Rs.3000+Certificate; 3rd prize: Rs. 1500+Certificate

Date: 29th of October 2018 (1 pm onwards)

SCHOOL-O-TECH :

To inspire the school students of this region to step into the practical world of technology TECHCRACY’18 brings to you SCHOOL-O-TECH. It is a science model making competition for the students upto class 12 based on innovative ideas/concepts of modern science and technology.

Rules:

    -Each group should be consisted of a maximum of 4 members.

    -Rewards will be given to best three groups.

    -Certificate of participants will be provided to each group.

Prize:

1st prize: Rs. 3000+Certificate; 2nd prize: Rs.2000+Certificate; 3rd prize: Rs. 1000+Certificate

Date: 30th of October 2018
